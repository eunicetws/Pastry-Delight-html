FAQs

Are there any artificial sugar?

All are products are made from natural sugar or natural sweetener.

How long can our dessert stay fresh?

Best to be consume within 2 days. We do not add any preservatives in our product.

Where to find out more about the seasonal menu?

Pastry Delights do provide seasonal menu. Click seasonal menu to find out more.

How far in advance do I need to place my order?

Order should be placed at least 2 days before the delivery date.

Can I adjust the sweetness or customize my cake?

You can customize your order by filling in additional notes when you check out.

Are the products Pet Friendly?

Definetely NO!. For safety purpose, please keep our products away from your pet.

Are orders refundable?

All orders are non-refundable.

Are our cakes suitable for specific diets or allergies?

Please note that many of our products contain eggs, gelatin, dairy, and sugar. As a result, individuals following specific diets such as those for diabetics, vegans, or keto may find them unsuitable. Additionally, some of our products include nuts and honey as ingredients. Therefore, individuals with allergies to these items should exercise extra caution when consuming our products.
